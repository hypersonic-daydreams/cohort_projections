6. Methodology Notes

  • Migration rate perturbation: All county-age-sex migration rates are multiplicatively scaled by the stated factor.
  • Fertility rate perturbation: All age-specific fertility rates (ASFRs) are multiplicatively scaled.
  • Survival rate perturbation: The death probability q = 1 - S is scaled by the stated percentage. This avoids pushing survival rates beyond 1.0 while maintaining proportional perturbation near the boundary.
  • SDC Bakken dampening: The flat post-averaging dampening factor applied to migration rates in Bakken oil counties (baseline = 0.6).
  • Mortality improvement rate: Annual compound reduction in death probability for the M2026 method (baseline = 0.5%/year).
  • Convergence schedule: The M2026 method normally ramps from recent to long-term migration rates over the projection horizon. These tests override the schedule to use a single window throughout.
  • Metrics: State % error = (projected - actual) / actual * 100. County MAPE = mean of |county % errors| across all 53 counties.
  • Origin averaging: Tornado and sweep charts average results across origin years 2015, 2020 to reduce origin-specific noise.
