Figure 2: International migration to North Dakota, 2010–2024. Panel (A) shows annual migration
flows with a quadratic trend line. Panel (B) displays North Dakota’s share of total U.S. international
migration, with the solid line showing observed values and the dashed line indicating the HP
filter trend component (λ = 6.25). Vertical dotted lines mark identified structural breaks at 2020
