  planning today; data-driven draft assets need regeneration after the
  baseline-only rerun.

  applies the CBO additive migration adjustment and the -5% fertility
  adjustment.

- City/place projections are not part of this release.
- The downloadable data is one consolidated workbook (and a tidy CSV at final
  release) — easy for nontechnical users.

  who need exact values.
- The unadjusted trend-continuation path stays internal, and restricted/high
  growth are not part of the marketing package.
