# Draft Numbers For Layout

These are rounded draft values from the March 2026 exports. They are for copy
development, chart sizing, and table layout only.

Refresh every number after CF-001 is resolved and final production outputs are
approved.

## Statewide Scenario Range

| Scenario | 2025 | 2030 | 2040 | 2050 | 2055 | 2025-2055 change |
|----------|------|------|------|------|------|------------------|
| Restricted Growth | 799,000 | 784,000 | 812,000 | 827,000 | 828,000 | 29,000 |
| Baseline (Trend Continuation) | 799,000 | 810,000 | 848,000 | 874,000 | 882,000 | 83,000 |
| High Growth | 799,000 | 847,000 | 941,000 | 1,035,000 | 1,078,000 | 279,000 |

Layout takeaways:

- The baseline reaches about 882,000 residents by 2055.
- The 2055 scenario range runs from about 828,000 to 1.08 million residents.
- The spread is mainly a migration story, so scenario assumptions need to
  appear early in the PDF.
